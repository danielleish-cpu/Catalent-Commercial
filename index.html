Executive Summary

Why This Matters for Catalent's Commercial Team

Catalent has invested deeply in Salesforce and M365. But the intelligence that wins deals, prevents missteps, and accelerates proposals still lives in silos across dozens of systems, inboxes, and M&A-inherited platforms. Commercial teams are making decisions with partial information.

Glean connects all of it into a single AI-powered intelligence layer that gives your reps cross-division visibility, faster proposals, and smarter lead prioritization, on top of the tools you already use.

Pipeline Acceleration
Score and prioritize leads using data from Salesforce custom objects, Eloqua, Gong, and email. Cross-division customer lookup before every outreach.
⚡
Faster Proposals
